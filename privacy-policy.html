Privacy Policy

Checkolist

Last updated: 7 February 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Checkolist, operated by Giaro Apps (an individual developer based in India), collects, uses, processes, stores, and protects personal data when you use the Checkolist mobile application and related services.

By accessing or using Checkolist, you acknowledge that you have read and understood this Privacy Policy.

1. Who We Are

  • App Name: Checkolist
  • Developer / Operator: Giaro Apps (individual)
  • Country of operation: India
  • Support & Grievance Email: app.support+checkolist@giaro.site

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Account & Authentication Data

We collect information required to create and manage your account, including:

  • Email address (including Apple private relay email, if applicable)
  • Name (if provided)
  • Profile image (if uploaded)
  • Authentication identifiers from Apple Sign In and Google Sign In

2.2 Checklist & App Usage Data

  • Checklists and checklist items
  • Checklist status (created, in progress, completed)
  • Reminder schedules
  • Activity metrics such as total and completed checklists

2.3 AI-Generated Content & Third-Party Processing

Checkolist provides an AI-powered checklist generation feature using third-party AI services operated by OpenAI via API access.

  • User prompts are transmitted securely to OpenAI’s API for processing
  • Raw prompts are not permanently stored by Checkolist
  • Generated checklist content (structured data) may be stored in your account
  • Users may edit or delete AI-generated content at any time

Data sent via the OpenAI API is not used by OpenAI to train or improve their models. OpenAI acts solely as a data processor on our behalf. For more information, please review OpenAI’s Privacy Policy.

AI-generated content is provided for productivity assistance only and should not be considered professional, medical, legal, or financial advice.

2.4 Notifications

We collect device notification tokens and reminder schedules in order to send server-triggered checklist reminders and essential app notifications. Notifications can be disabled at any time through device or app settings.

2.5 Analytics

We currently use Firebase Analytics to understand app usage, performance, and stability. This may include anonymized or aggregated data such as device type, app version, and interaction events.

We do not use analytics data for advertising or sell such data to third parties. If additional analytics tools are introduced in the future, users will be notified prior to such changes.

3. Legal Basis for Processing (GDPR)

For users located in the European Economic Area (EEA) or the United Kingdom, we process personal data under the following legal bases:

  • Performance of a contract – account creation, checklist management, reminders
  • Consent – AI-generated checklists and notifications
  • Legitimate interest – app security, analytics, and service improvement

4. How We Use Your Information

  • Provide authentication and account management
  • Create, manage, and synchronize checklists
  • Send reminders and notifications
  • Generate AI-powered checklist content
  • Improve performance, reliability, and security
  • Comply with legal and regulatory obligations

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal data.

5. Data Storage & International Transfers

User data is stored on cloud infrastructure. Servers and databases may be located outside your country of residence. By using Checkolist, you explicitly consent to the storage and processing of your data in such locations.

6. Data Retention

We retain personal data only for as long as your account remains active or as necessary to provide the service. If an account remains inactive for a prolonged period, data may be deleted in accordance with reasonable retention policies, unless legally required to retain it.

7. Account Deletion

You may delete your account at any time from within the app. Upon deletion, your personal data, checklists, reminders, and AI-generated content are deleted immediately, except where retention is required by law.

8. Data Security

We use industry-standard security measures, including encryption in transit (TLS/HTTPS), access controls, and secure authentication practices. While we take reasonable steps to protect your data, no system can be guaranteed to be completely secure.

9. Children’s Privacy

Checkolist is intended for users aged 13 years and above. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children under 13.

10. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights to access, correct, delete, or withdraw consent for processing of your personal data. These rights may be exercised through the app or by contacting us.

11. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. For material changes, we will notify users via in-app notification or email. Continued use of the app after such notice constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

This policy may also apply to future web or cross-platform versions of Checkolist. Additional cookie or tracking disclosures will be provided if applicable.
