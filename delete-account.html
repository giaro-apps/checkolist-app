Delete Your Checkolist Account

Checkolist allows you to permanently delete your account and all associated data at any time. Please review the information below before proceeding.

Delete Account Within the App

You can delete your Checkolist account directly from within the app by following these steps:

  • Open the Checkolist app
  • Go to the Profile tab
  • Select Account Settings
  • Tap Delete Account
  • Confirm the deletion

What Data Is Deleted

When your account is deleted, all associated user data is permanently removed from our systems, including:

  • Account information (email and profile details)
  • All checklists, routines, and related content
  • Reminder and scheduling data
  • AI-generated checklist data associated with your account

Data Retention

We do not retain personal data after account deletion. Upon account deletion, your personal data, checklists, reminders, and AI-generated content are deleted immediately from our systems.

Important Notice

Account deletion is permanent and irreversible. Once deleted, your account and data cannot be restored.

For more information about how we handle your data, please review our Privacy Policy.
